Peasant's Plight

Unlike traditional games where civilian reactions are scripted or simplistic, this project utilized the A* pathfinding algorithm in the Godot engine to create dynamic, varied responses among villagers. These responses included running, grouping, warning others, hiding, and retrieving valuables.

Using the lightweight, compatible Godot engine, the team employed its built-in A* pathfinding and a Navigation Agent 2D system to facilitate complex civilian behaviors.Civilians not only flee from the dragon but also interact with each other, warn of dangers, and utilize the environment strategically (e.g., hiding in buildings). The villagers' behaviors are driven by various heuristics, like the proximity to threats and opportunities to gather objects even during a crisis.
