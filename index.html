Hi! I'm Mark McAteer, a Gameplay Programmer with a B.S. in Computer Science: Game Design from the University of California, Santa Cruz. I specialize in gameplay programming in Unreal Engine, particularly in combat systems and the Gameplay Ability System (GAS).

I have extensive experience with C, C++, Python, Git, and Unreal Engine, and I enjoy designing engaging gameplay mechanics that enhance player experience. My projects range from rogue-lite combat systems to AI-driven gameplay agents and even deep-learning-based interactive experiences.
